title: Conversation Driven Development
date: 2026-06-18
slug: conversation-driven-development
excerpt: This blog will remain human driven.
---
One of my goals with this website is to drive the entire design, development, and deployment process with AI agents. For better or worse, I'm building everything through conversations. Call it a hands-on learning experience that will help me grow with the times. 

The only thing I'm not using agents to build is this blog. Instead, I write the posts myself (I tend to write quickly) and upload the raw file. After that, the agent deploys it and makes sure it looks good on the page.
